Sorry this slipped off my radar - you asked for HL7 v2 device data samples.

As we all know, the fit between device data and V2 is not great. Observation reporting is heavily influenced by the history of being used first for labs, so device observations have been shoehorned in, without a very good way of representing measurement status in particular. So in IHE Patient Care Devices domain (PCD) we just do:

msmt-state-in-alarm gets put into OBX-8 - H or L for high or low alarm

the other "qualifiers" like invalid or questionable or demo data, we just handle by not sending the observation! Uninformative, but safer if we can't be confident that the receiving system will distrust it appropriately...

An example of device data representation is in the IHE Device Enterprise Communication profile PCD-01 (Patient Care Devices domain, first transaction defined. All the IHE PCD profiles are pointed to by:

http://ihe.net/Technical_Framework/index.cfm#pcd

And the DEC profiles in particular are covered in the Technical Framework Volume 2:

http://ihe.net/Technical_Framework/upload/IHE_PCD_TF_Vol2.pdf
